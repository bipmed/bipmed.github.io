# Accessing Data from BIPMed

Please, check the Rules for Data Access ([english](/docs/1_RulesDataAccessBIPMed.docx), [portuguese](/docs/1_RegrasAcessoDadosBIPMed.docx) or [spanish](/docs/1_ReglasDisponibilidadDadosBIPMed.docx) version), fill out the Research Data Use Agreement ([english](/docs/3_AgreementDataUseBIPMed.docx), [portuguese](/docs/3_AcordoUsoDadosBIPMed.docx) or [spanish](/docs/3_AcuerdoUsoDadosBIPMed.docx) version) and contact us for further details.

# Sharing Data with BIPMed

Please, fill out the  Agreement for Data Deposit into BIPMed Public Genomic Database file ([english](/docs/2_DepositDataBIPMed.docx), [portuguese](/docs/2_DepositoDadosBIPMed.docx) or [spanish](/docs/2_AlmacenamientoDadosBIPMed.docx) version) and contact us for further details.

You will need a Research Consent Form for each patient that agrees to participate in your project.  Please, see a model of the Research Consent Form ([english](/docs/4_TCLE_english.pdf), [portuguese](/docs/4_TCLE_D_Neuropsiquiatricas_portugues.doc) or [spanish](/docs/4_TCLE_espanol.doc) version).
